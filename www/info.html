Diet Problem Solver


  This app's main feature is to find the cheapest and most nutritious combination of foods that will satisy all the daily nutritional requirements of an individual. The problem is formulated as a linear program where the objective is to minimize cost and meet constraints but will satisfy the nutritional needs. The program included constraits that regulate the number of calories and amounts of vitamins, minerals, fats, sodium, and cholesterol in the diet. A certain food option may only have 0-10 servings.
